Projects

MindSupport

www.mindsupport.ai

A comprehensive online platform that allows users to engage with a supportive AI chatbot, who can help them take the first step in their mental health journey. MindSupport strives to empower individuals, foster mental health education, and provide additional support for those facing mental health challenges. The AI chatbot helps users find local mental health providers, navigate insurance coverage, and provides mental health resources and support through understanding conversation.

I used front-end development languages: HTML, JS, and CSS, to create a website and a rule-based AI system. The basic framework (i.e., the website and basic chatbot system) took approximately 18 hours to complete; however, I am continuing to work on this project to improve upon and create additional features.

Encourage Kindness

www.encouragekind.org

A blog created to broadcast the benefits of kindness, spread awareness about social problems, and encourage people to start thinking about how they're contributing to society. Experience

Undergraduate Research Assistant

Center for Healthy Minds

Worked with my team to investigate the qualitative effects of using a meditation app for four weeks for individuals with significiant symptoms of anxiety and depression. I transcribed and verified interviews to learn various aspects of users' experience within the app and maintain a medication practice. I am currently using NVivo for conducting deductive qualitative coding by annotating the answers in concise ways and coding based on the CFIR model to assess the effect of the app and the amount of meditation needed for reaching the ideal effects.
